Courier Support App Redesign

Couriers needed to resolve issues through an app — but the IA didn't match their mental models. I redesigned it using moderated interviews and card sorting.

The Situation

Couriers were losing trust in the support system.

Glovo's courier support app was the primary channel for resolving delivery issues. Couriers were abandoning mid-flow or escalating to phone support. The hypothesis: IA didn't match how couriers categorised their problems.

What I Did

Research first. Redesign after.

8 moderated interviews in Spain and Portugal. Open + closed card sorting with 40 couriers via Maze.

Moderated Interviews Open Card Sorting Closed Card Sorting Thematic Analysis
What We Found

Three mental models, not one.

1. Couriers group issues by urgency, not category type.
2. "Earnings" was always the first mental entry point.
3. Safety concerns had no clear entry point.
The Impact

A restructured IA that reflects how couriers think.

Reflection

Mental models beat category logic every time.
