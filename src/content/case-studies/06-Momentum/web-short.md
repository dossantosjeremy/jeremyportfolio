# Momentum — Preview Card

---

**When apps speak framework, users hear "this isn't for me."**

I led the UX research for Momentum — an AI-powered goal-achievement app. Fifteen participants across Spain, France, and the UK revealed that the product's core concept was sound, but its language was the barrier. Terms like "OKR" and "milestones" created immediate drop-off. The AI coaching dialogue, when it worked, was the experience users described as genuinely different.
