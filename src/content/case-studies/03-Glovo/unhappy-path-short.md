# Glovo — Unhappy Path (No Orders) — Preview Card

---

**Couriers built their own troubleshooting systems. Glovo had no idea.**

When couriers sat idle waiting for orders, the app offered nothing — no explanation, no guidance, no status. I researched how to communicate in that void. Nine interviews across Spain, Italy, and Romania revealed that experienced couriers had already developed effective coping strategies — but Glovo was completely out of the loop. The recommendations created a shared source of truth between couriers and support agents.
