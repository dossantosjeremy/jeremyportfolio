# Glovo — Courier Support Experience — Preview Card

---

**40,000 couriers. One broken IA. And a pattern that nobody expected.**

Couriers weren't using Glovo's support system because they couldn't find things — they were bypassing it strategically, choosing wrong options on purpose to avoid automation. Three interlocked research studies over two years redesigned the entire courier support experience and produced a 58% findability improvement.
