# Glovo — Courier Not Finding the Customer — Preview Card

---

**Couriers weren't avoiding the feature by mistake. They were avoiding it to save their lives.**

When customers were unreachable during deliveries, couriers were systematically misusing the "incorrect address" support option — triggering expensive human agent contacts — instead of using the customer-absent automation. 10 interviews revealed why: the automation's timer was too long, there was no revert option, and in some markets, waiting in place was a safety risk. The recommendations directly changed the feature's design priorities.
