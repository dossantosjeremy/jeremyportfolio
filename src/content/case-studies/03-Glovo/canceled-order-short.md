# Glovo — Returning a Canceled Order — Preview Card

---

**A real-world diary study, coordinated live across two markets, testing a feature the moment couriers used it for the first time.**

When orders were canceled after pickup, couriers had no clear guidance. I ran a WhatsApp-moderated diary study with 8 beta couriers in Portugal and Kenya — triggered by real canceled orders created by local operations — to validate a new return automation before full launch. The study validated the concept while surfacing two critical copy issues that were fixed before rollout.
