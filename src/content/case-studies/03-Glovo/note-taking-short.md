# Glovo — Research Ops: Note-Taking — Preview Card

---

**When the research process itself becomes the research question.**

Great research is routinely undermined by poor note-taking from observers who weren't briefed on what to capture. I interviewed 5 stakeholders, defined 2 personas, and built the infrastructure — template + Confluence documentation — that standardised note-taking across all of Glovo's UX Research sessions. A ResearchOps project that made every subsequent study more efficient.
