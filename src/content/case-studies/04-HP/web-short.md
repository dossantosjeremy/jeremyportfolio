# HP — Financing Discovery — Preview Card

---

**HP's financing was invisible — but not because it was hidden. Because nobody was looking for it.**

The team assumed financing helped customers *decide* what to buy. Research proved the opposite: customers discovered financing after they'd already decided, as a pleasant surprise. That single insight reframed HP's entire eCommerce financing strategy.
