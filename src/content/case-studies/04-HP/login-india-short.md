# Improving HP India's Login Experience

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP India · **Date:** December 2023 · **Method:** Evaluative research

HP India's login flow had elevated friction relative to other markets. This evaluative study assessed where Indian customers encountered barriers — including OTP delivery, password recovery, and session management — and identified the points of highest abandonment.

Findings were tailored for the Indian market context, accounting for device variability, connectivity patterns, and local authentication expectations.
