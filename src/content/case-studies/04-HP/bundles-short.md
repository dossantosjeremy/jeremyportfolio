# HP Bundles Research

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Method:** Discovery & evaluative research

HP's bundled product offerings (hardware + accessories + services) were underperforming relative to the opportunity. This study examined how customers perceived value in bundled offers — what combinations felt logical, what felt forced, and what price presentation drove or blocked conversion.

Research identified the bundle compositions and presentation formats most likely to drive incremental attach, with clear implications for the product catalogue and PDP merchandising strategy.
