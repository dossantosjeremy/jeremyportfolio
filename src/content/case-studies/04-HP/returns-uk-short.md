# Helping HP Customers Return Their Orders

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP UK · **Method:** Evaluative research · **Participants:** 24

HP's UK returns flow had high abandonment — customers were starting the return process and not completing it. This study with 24 UK participants mapped every friction point across the returns journey: from the moment a customer decided to return, through finding the returns policy, initiating the process, and reaching resolution.

Findings identified the specific steps causing drop-off and the language breakdowns that led to customer confusion, with prioritised design recommendations delivered to the HP UK product team.
