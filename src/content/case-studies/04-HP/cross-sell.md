# Identifying Cross-Sell Needs: STO vs CTO Buyer Behaviour

> **One-line hook**: HP's STO (Ship to Order) cross-sell modal was generating more revenue than the multi-page CTO (Configure to Order) experience. But was the experience right for both buyer types? The product team needed to know whether cross-sell should be standardised — or differentiated. I designed the research to find out.

---

## Key Questions

- Which cross-sell experience generates the most revenue per order?
- In which cross-sell experience do users add more accessories?
- Does buyer type (STO vs. CTO) predict cross-sell engagement patterns?
- What are the differences in mental model, expectation, and behaviour between the two segments?
