# Incentivising HP Customers to Register Online

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Date:** January 2024 · **Method:** Evaluative & motivational research

HP wanted to increase online product registration rates — a key signal for warranty validation, post-purchase engagement, and lifetime value. The study explored why customers skipped registration and what incentive structures or timing changes would shift behaviour.

The research targeted a measurable outcome: a 5% increase in registration completion. Findings mapped the motivational triggers and friction points across the post-purchase registration flow.
