# HP Subscriptions Discovery Research

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Method:** Discovery research

HP was exploring a subscription model for hardware and services. Before committing to a product direction, the team needed to understand the mental models customers already held about tech subscriptions — what they expected, what they feared, and what would make a hardware subscription feel different from a software one.

This discovery study mapped the opportunity space, surfacing the motivations and barriers that would need to be addressed in any subscription product experience.
