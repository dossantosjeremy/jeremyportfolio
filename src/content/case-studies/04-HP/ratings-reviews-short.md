# Incentivising HP Customers to Leave Ratings and Reviews

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Method:** Motivational & evaluative research

Customer reviews are a key conversion driver for HP's e-commerce business — but review submission rates were low. This study examined the motivations and barriers to leaving a review post-purchase, including timing, effort, perceived value, and trust in the platform.

Findings informed a redesigned review request flow, including optimal timing triggers and incentive structures that respect customer effort without feeling transactional.
