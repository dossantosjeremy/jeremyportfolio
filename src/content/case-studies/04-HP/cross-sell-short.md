# Identifying Cross-Sell Needs: STO vs CTO Buyer Behaviour

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Method:** Discovery research

HP's cross-sell strategy treated all buyers the same. This study distinguished two distinct purchase paths — Standard Configured (STO) and Custom Configured (CTO) buyers — and mapped how their needs, decision criteria, and product relationship differed at the point of cross-sell.

The research informed a segmented cross-sell approach, giving HP's product team a framework for presenting accessories and complementary products based on buyer type rather than a one-size-fits-all model.
