# Helping HP Mexico Customers Choose the Right Printer

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP Mexico · **Method:** Evaluative research · **Participants:** 8

HP's printer category page presented customers with dozens of options and minimal guidance. This study explored how Mexican customers navigated printer selection — what criteria they used, where they got confused, and what product information was missing at the point of decision.

Research surfaced the comparison signals that mattered most to Mexican buyers and the specific terminology gaps that caused premature drop-off from the category.
