# Designing for Customization: HP Product Configuration

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Method:** Discovery & evaluative research

HP's product configurator allowed customers to customise hardware specs — but configuration completion rates were lower than expected. This study explored how customers approached the configuration process: what choices felt manageable, where cognitive load became overwhelming, and what defaults reduced decision paralysis.

Findings were used to simplify the configuration interface and restructure the presentation of options for HP's CTO (custom-configured) buyer segment.
