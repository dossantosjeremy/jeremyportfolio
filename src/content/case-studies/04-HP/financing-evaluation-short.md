# Evaluating HP's New Financing Experience

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP · **Date:** October 2023 · **Method:** Evaluative research · **Participants:** 12

HP introduced a new financing option at checkout. This evaluative study assessed whether customers understood the offer, trusted it, and felt confident enough to act — across three core dimensions: convenience, transparency, and confidence.

Findings validated that the redesigned experience addressed the key trust signals customers needed. Research delivered actionable recommendations on information hierarchy, terminology clarity, and the specific moments where customers disengaged.
