# Helping HP India Customers Shop Through Promotions

**Role:** UX Researcher · **Client:** HP India · **Method:** Evaluative & generative · **Participants:** 20

HP India ran frequent promotional campaigns — but customers were abandoning at the point of redemption. This study uncovered what stopped Indian shoppers from completing promoted purchases, with a specific focus on payment method visibility and trust signals.

The key finding: displaying bank details on the Product Detail Page was the critical unlock. A design recommendation tied directly to this insight projected a **$2M annual revenue uplift** — one of the highest-impact findings across the HP engagement.
