# PSA Peugeot — Neural Up® — Preview Card

---

**Can engineered sound make driving physiologically safer?**

PSA's Neural Up® technology used rotational sound to induce driver relaxation. The assumption was that any variation would have a calming effect. It didn't. I designed the physiological + psychometric research protocol that revealed the truth — and saved the team from investing in the wrong feature variant.
