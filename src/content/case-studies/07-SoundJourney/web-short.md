# SoundJourney — Preview Card

---

**Spotify knows who you are. Travel apps don't know you exist.**

SoundJourney is a music-to-travel discovery app I designed and fully specified: connect your Spotify account, and the app generates exactly three personalised concert venue journeys anchored in your listening identity. I built the product strategy, the four-level matching algorithm, and an 8-week MVP roadmap — as a complete PRD ready for development.
