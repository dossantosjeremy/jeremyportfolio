# Renault — AI Living Characteristics — Preview Card

---

**When AI feels human, does it feel trustworthy — or uncanny?**

Renault R&D needed to know which AI personality characteristics would drive user attachment to in-car AI across their GEN1 and GEN3 platforms. I designed the first validated research framework for measuring user attachment to automotive AI — and the finding that changed their roadmap: it's utility, not personality, that determines attachment.
