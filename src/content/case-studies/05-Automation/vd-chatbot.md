# V+D Leads Conversion Chatbot & AI Agent

> **One-line hook**: V+D's website generated enquiries — but converting a curious visitor into a qualified prospect required multiple email exchanges. I built an AI agent that engages visitors, qualifies their project needs, and routes them into the onboarding pipeline automatically.

## Problem



![Chatbot decision flow — qualification logic, knowledge base retrieval and human escalation routing](images/automation-chatbot-flow.png)
*Chatbot decision flow — qualification logic, knowledge base retrieval and human escalation routing*Architecture and interior design enquiries are inherently high-variance — project type, scale, timeline, and budget vary enormously. V+D needed a way to qualify leads before scheduling a first call, without adding administrative overhead to the team.

---

## Solution



![AI agent n8n workflow — processes every lead with context-aware memory and automatic scheduling](images/automation-agent-workflow.png)
*AI agent n8n workflow — processes every lead with context-aware memory and automatic scheduling*An AI-powered website chatbot and lead qualification agent that:
- Engages website visitors with a structured but conversational dialogue
- Collects key project information (type, scope, timeline, location, budget range)
- Assesses lead quality based on collected data
- Routes qualified leads directly into V+D's onboarding pipeline (triggering the pre-engagement automation)
- Alerts the V+D team with a lead summary for immediate follow-up

The agent was designed to feel like a conversation with a V+D team member — not a form in disguise. The GPT-powered responses were prompted to match V+D's brand voice and design expertise.
